🔍 Indexing & Selection tsb

tsb provides pandas-style label-based and position-based selection via locSeries, ilocSeries, locDataFrame, ilocDataFrame, atDataFrame, and iatDataFrame. A Slice class replaces Python's start:stop:step syntax.

Slice — positional range selection

Pythontsb equivalent
s[1:4]new Slice(1, 4)
s[::2]new Slice(null, null, 2)
s[::-1]new Slice(null, null, -1)
s[-2:]new Slice(-2)
